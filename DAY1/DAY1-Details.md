
# DAY 1 — AB-100 Foundations & Agentic AI Introduction

## Overview

Day 1 introduces the core foundations of Microsoft AB-100 certification and enterprise Agentic AI concepts. Learners explore Microsoft AI ecosystem services, enterprise AI architecture patterns, AI governance basics, and hands-on implementation using Azure AI Foundry, Azure OpenAI, and Copilot Studio.
