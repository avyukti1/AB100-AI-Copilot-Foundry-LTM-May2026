# CapstoneProject1 — Enterprise Multi-Agent AI Business Platform

# Microsoft AB-100 Final Enterprise Capstone Project

---

# Project Overview

This capstone project demonstrates the design and implementation of an enterprise-grade multi-agent AI business platform using Microsoft AI ecosystem technologies.

The solution simulates a real enterprise environment where multiple AI agents collaborate intelligently to automate business workflows securely, efficiently, and at enterprise scale.

The project aligns with:
- Microsoft AB-100 Certification
- Enterprise AI Architecture
- Agentic AI Design Patterns
- Multi-Agent Systems
- AI Governance & Security
- Enterprise Workflow Automation

---

# Enterprise Business Scenario

A multinational organization wants to modernize enterprise operations using Agentic AI and intelligent automation.

Current challenges:
- Manual approval workflows
- IT support overload
- Delayed employee onboarding
- Slow procurement cycles
- Inefficient communication systems
- Lack of centralized AI governance
- Poor enterprise knowledge discovery

The enterprise wants to implement:
- AI-powered copilots
- Multi-agent orchestration
- Intelligent workflow automation
- Enterprise AI governance
- Secure AI architecture
